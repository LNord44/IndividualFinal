REVIEWS

Below are some of the shows/ movies, music, games, and books Luke has enjoyed enough to write a review about.

- Soul - Pixar's latest animated film. The best origional Pixar movie since Coco. You can watch it with a subsciption to Disney+. 9/10.

- The Last of Us Part II - This latest installment, created Naughty Dog Studios, saw massive review bombing before initial release. If you played the first game, you'll like this game. Ignore the haters. 10/10.

- Yandhi - Kanye West's unreleased album that was leaked in 2019, it is truly a shame this album never came to fruition. 7.5/10.

- The Mandolorian - The best Star Wars since the origional trilogy. Baby Yoda is quite cute. 9.5/10.

- The Alchemist - This book is a pretty cool metaphor for desires in life. It's a pretty easy read too. 6.5/10.
