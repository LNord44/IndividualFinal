BIOGRAPHY

(This photo was taken in Nordheim, Germany. A few hours South of Frankfurt, Germany.)

Luke Nordheim grew up in Pendleton County, Kentucky, one county South of NKU, and attended Pendleton County Highschool. There, he played American football, basketball, and played baritone saxophone in the band.

After high school, Luke attended the United States Air Force Academy for two years. During the coronavirus pandemic, he transferred back home to NKU. There, he is studying computer IT and computer science.

Some hobbies he enjoys doing are playing video games, watching sports, reading, playing pick-up basketball, and lifting weights.

Some other cool facts about Luke include being scuba certified and he enjoys fishing/ frog gigging (frog hunting).
